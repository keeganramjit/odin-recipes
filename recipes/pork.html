Slow Cooker BBQ Pulled Pork

Delicious, juicy pulled pork.

This slow cooker pulled pork recipe is incredibly easy to make at home if you have a slow cooker. All you need is some pork shoulder, some spices and several hours and you will have some of the best pulled pork you've ever had.

It's great to use in sandwiches, and you can make a lot of it at once that can be stored in the fridge to eat later.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bbq sauce
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 pork shoulder

Spice Mixture

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1/2 teaspon cayenne

Steps

  1. In the bowl of a slow cooker, whisk together the bbq sauce, chicken stock, apple cider vinegar. Add in the onions and garlic and stir to combine.
  2. Combine all of the spices in a small bowl and rub all around the pork including inside any folds in the meat. There should not be any remaining spices when finished.
  3. Place the pork in the slow cooker, cover, and cook on low until easily shredded with a fork, about 10 hours. remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred. 
  4. Ladle over some of the juices from the slow cooker. Top with extra bbq sauce and toss. 
  5. Serve on toasted brioche buns with coleslaw if desired.
