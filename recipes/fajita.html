Chicken Fajita Rice

Creamy and easy fajita rice.

An easy and healthy recipe if you're craving fajitas without having to go through too much trouble. Make it all using a rice cooker and one pan.

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken breast (sliced)
  • 2 medium bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1 small red onion (sliced)
  • squeeze of one lime
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 cups cooked rice (about 1 rice cup uncooked)
  • 1/3 cup fat free greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1/3 cup shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Add pepper, onion, chicken, lime, seasonings to a bowl and mix.
  2. Add it to a pan and cook until chicken is cooked, about 6-8 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Add cooked rice, yogurt, cheese, and hot sauce and mix until combined.
